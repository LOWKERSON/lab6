100 ft Waves

My favorite part of my Hawaii trip was seeing the 100ft waves up north in Maui. The beach is known as Jaws, but its official name is Pe'ahi. When I went to see the waves with my friend, we caught an event where we saw surfboarders riding the waves. Hearing the mass of the waves hitting the rocks on the shore was thrilling. Koko Crater Trail

My second favorite place from the trip to Hawaii was hiking the Koko Crater Trail with my friend. The trail features abandoned railway ties that lead to an incredible summit of 1,208 feet. Walking the trail was long and hard. It was like walking a mountain of stairs. But the view was worth it, and it also greatly enhanced my stamina. My friend is in the active military, and walking the trail took only 45 minutes. I took an hour and a half to walk. The average time to complete the trial was 1 hour. At the top, you can enter an abandoned bunker where the trolly used to station.

Hawaii Is A Great Place To Visit

I had a blast exploring Maui, Hawaii. It has a beautiful ocean scenery and vegetation. We went there during the winter, expecting it to be cold but hot and humid. We only had a week to explore the entire island and managed to explore in 4 days. My tip on coming to Honolulu is to take the bus for transportation. It was cost effective, and they have one of the best transit routes in the U.S. Its a great vacation spot for any occasion.
